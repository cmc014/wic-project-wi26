Aquarius


Aquarius is usually ahead of whatever is currently trending. They have strong opinions and don't need anyone to agree with them. They can come across as detached but they care more than they show. They get bored easily and move on fast.

Pisces


Pisces picks up on the energy in a room before anything is said. They feel things deeply and take on other people's emotions without realizing it. Creative, a little all over the place, and genuinely one of the most empathetic signs.

Aries


Aries moves fast and figures it out on the way. They are direct, sometimes impatient, and almost always the first person to say what everyone else is thinking. They bring a lot of energy to a room and they know it.

Taurus


Taurus knows what they like and they do not need to explain it. They take their time with everything and the end result is usually better for it. Reliable, a little stubborn, and genuinely good taste across the board.

Gemini


Gemini is interested in everything and everyone and it is completely genuine. They change their mind a lot because they are always taking in new information. Funny, quick, and way more thoughtful than people give them credit for.

Cancer


Cancer remembers everything. The conversation, the vibe, the small details nobody else noticed. They care a lot about the people in their life and they show it in quiet ways. The person you want around when something actually goes wrong.

Leo


Leo is warm, generous, and naturally draws people in. They have high standards and they apply them to themselves first. They like being recognized for the work they put in, which is fair because they actually put it in.

Virgo


Virgo notices the thing that is slightly off and usually does not say anything until the right moment. They think carefully before they speak and they mean what they say. High standards, good at most things, hard on themselves about it.

Libra


Libra has a good eye for most things. Music, fashion, people, how a room feels. They take a long time to make decisions because they genuinely see every side. Easy to be around and better at reading a situation than most people.

Scorpio


Scorpio picks up on things most people miss and keeps it to themselves. They are selective about who they trust and fully committed once they do. They do not do surface level in anything, music, friendships, opinions, none of it.

Sagittarius


Sagittarius is always onto the next thing. They get genuinely obsessed with something new every few months and mean it every time. Honest sometimes to a fault, hard to keep up with, and good at making any situation more interesting.

Capricorn


Capricorn is focused in a way that is hard to explain until you see it in action. They work quietly and take it seriously and they are usually further along than people realize. Funny when you get to know them but it takes a minute to get there.
